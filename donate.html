Donation questions?

What happens after I scan the QR code?

After scanning, your UPI app will open with the payment details pre-filled. Complete the payment and email us the transaction screenshot for confirmation and receipt.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Yes! You can choose a specific program area, or select "General Fund" to let us allocate where the need is greatest.

How do I track the impact of my donation?

Monthly donors receive detailed impact reports. All donors get quarterly updates showing exactly how funds were used.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use secure UPI payments through QR codes. Your banking information is processed directly by your UPI app.
